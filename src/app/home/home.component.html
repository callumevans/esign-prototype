What is PCI

Customers are legally entitled to be given certain information before entering into a finance agreement. ... For regulated consumer hire agreements, and some regulated credit agreements, the form and content of the pre-contract information is prescribed by the Consumer Credit (Disclosure of Information) Regulations 2004

What is Hire Purchase

Hire purchase is a way to finance buying a new or used car. You (usually) pay a deposit and pay off the value of the car in monthly instalments, with the loan secured against the car. This means you don't own the vehicle until the last payment is made.
