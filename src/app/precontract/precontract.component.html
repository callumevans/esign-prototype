4. Other important legal aspects
Right of Withdrawal You have the right to withdraw from the Agreement before the end of 14 days beginning with the day after the day on which we advise you that the Agreement has been made.
Early repayment You have the right to repay the credit early at any time in full or partially. If you do make a partial settlement, we will contact you and, taking effect from the next payment date, you may choose to either (i) reduce the amount of your future payments but keep the term of the Agreement the same or (ii) reduce the term of the Agreement but keep the amount of your future payments the same. Any part settlement will be applied against arrears and charges before reducing future payments.
Consultation with a Credit Reference Agency If we decide not to proceed with the Agreement on the basis of information received from a credit reference agency, we will notify you of our decision and will inform you that it has been reached on the basis of such information and of the particulars of the agency.
Right to a draft credit agreement You have the right to obtain, upon request, a copy of the draft Agreement free of charge, unless we are at the time unwilling to enter into the Agreement.
